Frequently Asked Questions

What are your requirements for new models?

We look for individuals with unique looks, professionalism, and dedication. Height requirements vary by division, but we value diversity and authenticity above all.

Do you charge models any fees?

Legitimate modeling agencies work on commission. We do not charge upfront fees for representation.

How can I book a model?

Contact our booking department with your project details, and we'll match you with the perfect talent from our roster.

Do you work internationally?

Yes! We have partnerships with agencies worldwide and regularly place models in international markets.

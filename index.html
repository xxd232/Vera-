Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to become a model?

Fill out our registration form above with your details and portfolio. Our team reviews all applications within 5 business days.

What are your agency fees?

We work on a commission basis. Models keep 80% of their earnings, and we take 20% to cover representation and booking services.

Do I need prior experience?

No! We work with models at all experience levels, from beginners to seasoned professionals.

What are the height requirements?

Requirements vary by category. Fashion models typically need to be 5'8" or taller, but we have opportunities for all heights in commercial modeling.
